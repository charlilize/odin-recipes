Quinoa Salad

A healthy meal filled with lots of vegetables. Vegan-friendly and seriously delicious. You'd want to make this again. It tastes better the longer it sits.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rinsed uncooked quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cup small diced cucumber
  • 3/4 cup drained and rinsed Garbanzo beans
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped parsley
  • 1/2 cupjuiced lemon
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine the ingredients for the quinoa in a small saucepot. Bring to a boil, reduce to a gentle simmer, cover, and let cook for 15 minutes or until the water is absorbed. Spread onto a baking sheet and let cool.
  2. While quinoa is cooking, prepare mise en place for all other ingredients. Combine ingredients for the salad in a large mixing bowl. Mix the dressing ingredients together in a separate bowl.
  3. Add the cooled quinoa and dressing to the large salad bowl. Mix to combine.
