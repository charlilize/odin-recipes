Red Velvet Cake

Create a sweet, truly red cake with frosting of just the right amount of sweetness. Perfect for gifting and parties. You won't be able to put this down after a bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 box Red Velvet Cake mix
  • 3/4 cup sour cream at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 large eggs at room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Grease and flour a Bundt pan.
  2. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl on low then to medium
  3. Pour into pan and bake for 45-50 minutes
