Inspired by Olive Garden's shrimp alfredo, this pasta is super yummy. Creamy and fulfilling, it will leave you wanting for more. Good source of protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb fettuccine
  • 1 lb large raw peeled & deveined shrimp
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 stick butter
  • 2 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1.5 cups milk
  • 1.5 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmeasan cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

‚è≤Ô∏è Steps


  1. Cook paste according to package directions.
  2. While pasta cooks, In a skillet, add olive oil and 2 tbsp butter to a large skillet over medium high heat. Once butter is melted, add shrimp and sauce for 1-2 min on each side until opaque. Remove from pan and set aside in a bowl and toss with Italian seasoning. Lightly cover.
  3. Once noodles are done ‚Äì In a saucepan over medium-high heat, Add remaining butter and garlic. Cook for 1 minute, stirring occasionally.
  4. Lower to medium heat and whisk in flour and quickly add milk, heavy cream, Parmesan and Romano cheese to saucepan and stir frequently until the cheese melts and incorporates into the sauce and it begins to simmer and thicken. Add salt & pepper and whisk to combine. Remove from heat.
  5. Toss pasta with Alfredo sauce. Top with shrimp and garnish with Italian seasoning and fresh parsley
